Introduction

Cyber security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks. It involves tools, policies, and practices to safeguard information from unauthorized access. With increasing use of the internet, the threat of cybercrime has also grown rapidly. Cyber security ensures safety, privacy, and trust in the digital world.

importance of cyber security > Cyber security is important because personal, financial, and business data are at constant risk. Hackers often try to steal sensitive information for illegal purposes. A strong cyber defense protects not only data but also reputation. Without proper security, even large organizations can suffer huge losses

The dreams of yesterday are the hopes of today and the reality of tomorrow. Science has not yet mastered prophecy. We predict too much for the next year and yet far too little for the next ten.
